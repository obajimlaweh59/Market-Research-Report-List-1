<p><p>Chemical Enhanced Oil Recovery (EOR/IOR) involves using chemical agents to improve oil extraction from reservoirs. Key market types include water-soluble polymers that increase viscosity to improve sweep efficiency, surfactants that reduce interfacial tension for better oil displacement, and polymer gels that stabilize and control fluid flow. Biopolymers offer environmentally friendly alternatives, while alkaline chemicals enhance oil recovery by reacting with acids in crude oil. Other emerging technologies and materials also contribute to the EOR/IOR landscape, refining extraction processes.</p></p>
