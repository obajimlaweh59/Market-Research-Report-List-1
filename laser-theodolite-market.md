<p><strong>The Laser Theodolite Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transit Theodolite</li><li>Non-transit Theodolite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser theodolite market consists of two primary types: transit and non-transit theodolites. Transit theodolites allow for the measurement of angles in both horizontal and vertical planes, featuring a rotating telescope for enhanced precision in surveying and construction. Conversely, non-transit theodolites, often simpler in design, provide fixed telescopes for basic angle measurements without rotation. Both types utilize laser technology to enhance accuracy and visibility, making them essential tools in various applications such as engineering, surveying, and architectural projects.</p></p>
