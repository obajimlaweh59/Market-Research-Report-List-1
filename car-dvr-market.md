<p><p>The Car DVR market is primarily categorized into two types: single-channel and dual-channel. Single-channel DVRs consist of one camera, typically mounted at the front, capturing only the forward view, making them a cost-effective choice for basic monitoring. Dual-channel DVRs, on the other hand, feature two cameras—front and rear—providing comprehensive coverage and enhanced safety by recording incidents from multiple angles. This dual approach caters to consumers seeking greater awareness and security while driving.</p></p>
