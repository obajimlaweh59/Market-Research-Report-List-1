<p><p>The pharmaceutical grade gelatin powder market is primarily categorized into two types: bone gelatin and skin gelatin. Skin gelatin, derived from animal hides, is more versatile and widely used in the production of encapsulations, active ingredient delivery systems, and food-grade products. Both types serve critical roles in healthcare and pharmaceutical formulations, ensuring product efficacy and safety.</p></p>
