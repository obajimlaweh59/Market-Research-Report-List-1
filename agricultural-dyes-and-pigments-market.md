<p><p>The agricultural dyes and pigments market is categorized into two primary types: dyes and pigments. Dyes are soluble colorants used primarily in fertilizers and seed coatings to enhance visibility and identification, promoting efficient use and application. Pigments, on the other hand, are insoluble colorants that impart color to various agricultural products, such as plastics and equipment, offering UV protection and aesthetic appeal. Both types play a crucial role in improving product performance and marketability in the agricultural sector.</p></p>
