<p><ul><li>Thermal Printer</li><li>Impact/Dot Matrix Printer</li><li>Inkjet Printer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ethernet receipt printer market comprises three main types: thermal, impact (dot matrix), and inkjet printers. Thermal printers use heat to produce prints, making them fast and efficient for receipts, while impact printers create impressions by striking an inked ribbon against paper, suitable for multi-part forms. Inkjet printers utilize liquid ink for high-quality images but are less common for receipts due to slower speeds. Each type serves different business needs, balancing speed, print quality, and operational costs.</p></p>
