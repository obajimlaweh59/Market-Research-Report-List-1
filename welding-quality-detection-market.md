<p><ul><li>Destructive Detection</li><li>Non-destructive Detection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The welding quality detection market is divided into two main types: destructive detection and non-destructive detection. Destructive detection involves testing methods that result in the destruction of the welded material, such as tensile testing or impact testing, to assess weld integrity. Conversely, non-destructive detection methods, such as ultrasonic testing, radiographic testing, and magnetic particle inspection, evaluate weld quality without compromising the integrity of the material. Both methods are crucial for ensuring safety and reliability in various industries.</p></p>
