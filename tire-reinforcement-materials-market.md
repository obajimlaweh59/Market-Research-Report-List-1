<p><p>Tire reinforcement materials include various types that enhance tire strength and performance. Steel cords offer high tensile strength and puncture resistance, making them ideal for heavy-duty tires. Polyester provides excellent flexibility and resistance to fatigue, commonly used in passenger vehicle tires. Rayon, while less common, contributes to improved durability and stability. Nylon is known for its abrasion resistance and impact strength, often used in performance tires. Each material plays a crucial role in optimizing tire safety, longevity, and performance.</p></p>
