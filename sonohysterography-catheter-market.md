<p><p>The Sonohysterography Catheter market comprises two main types: Single Lumen and Double Lumen catheters. Single Lumen Sonohysterography Catheters feature a single passage for saline infusion, used primarily for evaluating uterine abnormalities. In contrast, Double Lumen Sonohysterography Catheters have two separate channels, allowing simultaneous saline infusion and fluid drainage.
