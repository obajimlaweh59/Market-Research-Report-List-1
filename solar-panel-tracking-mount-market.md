<p><strong>The Solar Panel Tracking Mount Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Axis Tracking Mounts</li><li>Dual Axis Tracking Mounts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar panel tracking mount market primarily consists of two types: single-axis and dual-axis tracking mounts. Single-axis tracking mounts adjust panels along one axis, typically following the sun's daily movement from east to west, optimizing energy capture while maintaining a simpler design and lower cost. Conversely, dual-axis tracking mounts adjust both horizontally and vertically, enabling panels to track the sun’s changing position throughout the year, maximizing solar energy production but at a higher complexity and investment.</p></p>
