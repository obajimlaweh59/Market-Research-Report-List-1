<p><strong>The Plastic For Additive Manufacturing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resin Based</li><li>Powder Based</li><li>Filament</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic additive manufacturing market encompasses three main types: resin-based, powder-based, and filament. Resin-based materials are utilized in processes like stereolithography (SLA), allowing precise, detailed prints. Powder-based materials, often used in selective laser sintering (SLS), offer durability and are suitable for complex geometries. Filament materials are primarily associated with fused deposition modeling (FDM), where thermoplastic filaments are melted and deposited layer by layer. Each type serves distinct applications, ranging from prototyping to final product manufacturing.</p></p>
