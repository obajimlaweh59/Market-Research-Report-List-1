<p><p>Ultra-High Efficiency Toilets (UHETs) are designed to reduce water consumption while maintaining strong flushing performance. The market can be categorized into two main types: single-flush and dual-flush. Single-flush UHETs use a consistent amount of water per flush, typically around 1.28 gallons or less, suitable for most waste disposal needs. In contrast, dual-flush UHETs offer two flush options—one for liquid waste and another for solid waste—allowing users to choose the appropriate water volume, leading to further water savings.</p></p>
