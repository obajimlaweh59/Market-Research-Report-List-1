Key market types include refrigerated containers, which are mobile storage units equipped for maintaining specific temperatures during transport. Blast freezers rapidly freeze products, preserving their integrity and extending shelf life. Chillers provide controlled cooling for fresh goods, ensuring they remain at optimal temperatures. Cold rooms offer spacious environments for bulk storage, ideal for perishable items like fruits, vegetables, dairy, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
