<p><strong>The Solid Fat Content Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Method</li><li>Indirect Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Solid Fat Content (SFC) Analyzer market is segmented into two primary methods: direct and indirect. The direct method involves the actual measurement of fat content by techniques such as nuclear magnetic resonance (NMR) or differential scanning calorimetry (DSC), providing precise results. Conversely, the indirect method estimates solid fat content through empirical relations or models using parameters like temperature or density, offering quicker assessments. Both methods cater to different industry needs, ensuring accurate fat content analysis in food production and quality control.</p></p>
