<p><p>The off-line LED drivers market is categorized into two primary types: DC power and AC power. DC power drivers convert the input voltage directly to a stable DC output, making them suitable for direct connection to LED modules. In contrast, AC power drivers manage alternating current inputs, efficiently transforming them into the required voltage and current to power LEDs. Both types are essential for enabling energy-efficient lighting solutions, catering to various applications in commercial and residential sectors.</p></p>
