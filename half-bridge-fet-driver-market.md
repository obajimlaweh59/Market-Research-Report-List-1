<p><p>The Half-Bridge FET Driver market comprises two main types: Non-Isolated Drivers and Isolated Drivers. Non-Isolated Drivers are designed for applications where the driver and the power stage share a common ground, offering simplicity and lower cost. In contrast, Isolated Drivers provide electrical separation between the control and power stages, enhancing safety and noise immunity, making them suitable for high-voltage applications. Both types play crucial roles in efficiently driving MOSFETs and IGBTs in power conversion and motor control systems.</p></p>
