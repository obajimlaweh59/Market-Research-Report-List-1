<p><p>Subsea valves are crucial components in the oil and gas industry, enabling efficient flow control and safety underwater. Subsea ball valves provide simple on/off functionality with minimal pressure drop. Subsea check valves prevent backflow, ensuring one-directional flow. Subsea gate valves allow full flow and are often used for isolation. Subsea needle valves offer precise throttling and flow control in low-flow applications. Each type serves specific operational needs, enhancing performance and safety in subsea environments.</p></p>
