<p><p>The Thermal Transfer Overprinters (TTO) market includes two primary types: Multi Head and Single Head Thermal Transfer Overprinters. Multi Head TTO systems feature multiple print heads, allowing for simultaneous printing on various packaging materials, enhancing efficiency and throughput. In contrast, Single Head TTO systems utilize a single print head, making them more suitable for lower volume applications or simpler printing tasks. Both types are essential in industries like food, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics for high-quality product coding and labeling.</p></p>
