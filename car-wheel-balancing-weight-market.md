<p><p>Car wheel balancing weights are essential for maintaining smooth vehicle operation by ensuring even weight distribution on wheels. The market features two main types: clip-on and adhesive weights. Clip-on weights attach to the rim's outer edge, offering easy installation and adjustment. They’re commonly used in professional settings. Adhesive weights, on the other hand, stick directly to the wheel's interior surface, providing a more discreet appearance. This type is often preferred for alloy wheels where aesthetics are a priority. Both types effectively enhance driving performance and tire longevity.</p></p>
