<p><p>Search Engine Marketing (SEM) services encompass various pricing models. CPT (Cost Per Time) charges advertisers based on the duration ads are displayed. CPA (Cost-Per-Acquisition) focuses on costs associated with acquiring a customer, charging only when a desired action is completed. CPC (Cost-Per-Click) bills advertisers for each click their ad receives, while CPM (Cost-Per-Thousand-Impressions) charges per thousand times an ad is shown. Other models may include hybrid pricing strategies or pay-per-lead approaches, catering to specific marketing objectives.</p></p>
