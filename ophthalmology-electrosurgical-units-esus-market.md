<p><p>Ophthalmology Electrosurgical Units (ESUs) are essential tools in eye surgeries, employing high-frequency electrical currents for tissue cutting and coagulation. The market consists of monopole ESUs, which utilize a single active electrode for surgery, ideal for precision cutting; bipolar ESUs, using both active and return electrodes for better control and reduced tissue damage; and vessel sealing units that effectively seal blood vessels, preventing hemorrhage. Each type serves specific surgical needs, enhancing safety and efficiency in ophthalmic procedures.</p></p>
