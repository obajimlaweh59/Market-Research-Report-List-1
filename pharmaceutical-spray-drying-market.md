<p><strong>The Pharmaceutical Spray Drying Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressure Nozzles</li><li>Two-Fluid Nozzles</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmaceutical spray drying market is categorized based on nozzle types, primarily featuring pressure nozzles and two-fluid nozzles. Pressure nozzles utilize a pump to create a fine mist of liquid, ensuring precise droplet formation and consistent drying. In contrast, two-fluid nozzles mix the liquid feed with a compressed gas, typically air, to atomize the solution effectively. Both types are crucial for producing high-quality pharmaceutical powders, enhancing product stability, bioavailability, and facilitating optimal drug delivery formulations.</p></p>
