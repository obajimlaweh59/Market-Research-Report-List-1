<p><ul><li>Natural System</li><li>Synthesis System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydrogel-based drug delivery system market is classified into two primary types: natural and synthetic systems. Natural hydrogels are derived from biopolymers like alginate, chitosan, and gelatin, offering biocompatibility and biodegradability, making them suitable for various medical applications. Synthetic hydrogels, on the other hand, are engineered from polymers, allowing for tailored properties such as controlled release and stability. Both systems provide unique advantages, enabling targeted drug delivery, sustained release, and improved therapeutic efficacy in diverse biomedical fields.</p></p>
